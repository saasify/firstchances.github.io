Gen E: Equity generation

Second chances are an opportunity to start over. Yet, millions of people lack FirstChances at life inheriting a legacy of economic and social disadvantage. Girls who face financial and social barriers to drop out after high school perpetuate inequities. College education empowers these young women to break the cycle by gaining confidence and creating new opportunities to build brighter futures for themselves and their families.

#BreakTheCycle

About FirstChances

FirstChances creates brighter futures and career opportunities for young women from disadvantaged socio-economic backgrounds through fully funded college education, support and mentoring.
